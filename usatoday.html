USA TODAY

2013 - 2015

Gannett owns over one hundred newspapers and television stations, each with their own websites. The company supported several content management systems (CMS), some of which lacked basic digital publishing features. USA Today was Gannett's largest property, and their system was notorious for causing issues in the newsroom.

  • Goal

    Create a single publishing platform for all Gannett news sites.

  • Challenge

    How might we reduce the time it takes to publish breaking news across Gannett properties?

  • The Team

    We were eight developers, two product managers, and one designer.

  • Approach

    Interview journalists to learn how they write stories and usability test interactive prototypes.

User Research

We observed and interviewed Gannett journalists around the country to design a story editor with an inviting interface for publishing breaking news more quickly. We learned that being able to craft SEO-friendly headlines and publishing stories to multiple pages were all considered vital features for user adoption.

Sharing Photos

Our team designed an image upload service that could tag and process large collections of images. We used an assumptions tracker to test how well journalists understood the interface and validated that users could find, crop, and share images across newsrooms.

Outcome

After releasing Presto to the USA TODAY newsroom, we rolled it out to all Gannett properties nationwide. Our product gave each site similar publishing capabilities, and enabled content sharing across the largest local news network in the United States.
